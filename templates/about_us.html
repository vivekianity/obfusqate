About ObfusQate

ObfusQate focuses on leveraging quantum computing primitives to develop an innovative obfuscation technique aimed at enhancing the security of quantum programs.

ObfusQate is an advanced automated obfuscation framework specifically designed for quantum programs and hybrid quantum-classical programs. By leveraging the unique properties of quantum mechanics, ObfusQate implements sophisticated obfuscation techniques that aim to enhance the security and privacy of quantum circuits and algorithms. These techniques help to obscure the underlying logic and data flow of quantum programs, making it significantly more challenging for unauthorized parties to reverse - engineer or tamper with the code. ObfusQate's innovative approach not only strengthens the security of quantum computing applications but also contributes to the broader field of cybersecurity by addressing emerging threats in the quantum computing landscape.
